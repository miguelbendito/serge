Our Services

The Hamptons, Long Island, and New York City


We provide private chef and dining services throughout The Hamptons, Long Island, and New York City, bringing world-class cuisine and hospitality to your home, vacation rental, or event space.

Our services are designed to suit any occasion, theme, or group dynamic with effortless adaptability. Hosting an intimate family-style dinner after a day at the beach? We’ll create a relaxed, comforting meal.

Planning a high-stakes corporate dinner in Manhattan? Our impeccable service and elevated cuisine will leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re celebrating, unwinding, or entertaining, we tailor every experience to make you feel at home—while delivering a level of luxury that is anything but ordinary.

Our Services Include

  • Family-Style Dining – Full-course meals served casually for a warm, communal experience.
  • Elevated Plated Dinners – Elegant 3–4 course meals with refined service.
  • Tasting Menus with Wine Pairings – Exquisite 5–8 course experiences curated with artisan wines.
  • Tapas-Style Dining – A variety of small plates for a dynamic, social meal.
  • Passed Appetizer Receptions – Heavy hors d’oeuvres perfect for cocktail-style events.
  • Small Bites & Crafted Cocktails – Impress your guests with elevated flavors in a refined setting.
  • Full-Week Chef Services – Let us handle all your meals for a stress-free getaway.
  • Grocery & Pantry Stocking – Arrive to a fully stocked kitchen with everything you need.

From casual gatherings to grand celebrations, we bring exceptional food, flawless execution, and effortless luxury to every event.
